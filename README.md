## The four natures of emergence

- 🟢 **natural** — flesh, blood, and the living world *(Samus, Ridley, Kraid, Draygon, Crocomire, the Space Pirates, the Etecoons & Dachora, Zebes)*
- 🟣 **ethereal** — the life-draining Metroid, the phantom *(the Metroids, Phantoon)*
- 🟡 **spiritual** — ancient sages, the sacrifice *(the Chozo, the Baby Metroid)*
- 🔵 **electrical** — a brain wired into the planet *(Mother Brain)*
